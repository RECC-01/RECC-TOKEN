# RECC TOKEN

**Reserve Economy Crypto Coin (RECC)**

RECC is a fixed-supply, value-based crypto asset inspired by Bitcoin’s monetary philosophy,
designed as a long-term digital reserve for the **RECC-01 / RECCNETWORK Blockchain ecosystem**,
with full EVM compatibility.

---

## Overview

**The Reserve Economy Crypto Coin (RECC)** is the native digital reserve asset of the RECCNETWORK Blockchain.
It is conceived as a foundational monetary unit focused on value preservation, economic stability,
and long-term vision rather than short-term utility.

RECC is built to function as a core reserve asset for:
- Blockchain exchange operations
- Decentralized finance (DeFi)
- Future financial infrastructure
- Purchases, settlements, and payments within the RECC ecosystem

---

## Key Properties

- Fixed Supply: **100,000,000 RECC**
- Decimals: **18**
- No minting
- No burning
- No admin control
- Immutable monetary rules
- Store-of-Value design

---

## Monetary Philosophy

RECC is **not** a utility token.  
RECC is **not** a governance token.  

RECC is a **reserve-value digital asset**, inspired by Bitcoin’s economic model,
designed to act as a long-term store of value within a sovereign blockchain ecosystem.

---

## Origin of the Name

The name **RECC** originates from the initials of its creator:

**Ricardo Enrique Catari Castillo**

The acronym represents both personal authorship and the broader vision of building
an independent, sovereign digital economic reserve for the RECCNETWORK Blockchain.

---

## Design Principles

- Fixed and immutable supply
- No inflation mechanisms
- No centralized control
- Long-term monetary vision
- Transparency and simplicity by design
